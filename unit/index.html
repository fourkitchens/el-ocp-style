Optional

Experts, Fieldwork & Service

  • Fieldwork: Visit local and school libraries, history museums with a focus on U.S. history and segregation
  • Experts: Learn from people who have traveled to other countries and encountered reading superheroes (e.g., teachers who have worked abroad, Peace Corps volunteers, etc.)

Extensions

  • Geography: Research about countries mentioned in reading; locating countries on a map
  • Art: Students create portraits of reading superheroes, or portray themselves as reading superheroes.
